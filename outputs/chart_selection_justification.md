# Chart Selection Justification

## Sales Trend

**Business Question:** How are sales changing over time?

**Chart Type:** Line Chart

**Reason:** Line charts effectively display trends and seasonal patterns over time.

**Fields Used**

* Columns: Order Date
* Rows: Sales
* Color: Profit

**Design Principle:** Continuous timeline with minimal clutter.

**Mistake Avoided:** Avoided bar charts for time-series analysis.

---

## Regional Performance

**Business Question:** Which regions perform best?

**Chart Type:** Horizontal Bar Chart

**Reason:** Bar charts provide easy comparison across regions.

**Fields Used**

* Region
* Sales
* Profit (Color)

**Design Principle:** Sorted descending for faster comparison.

**Mistake Avoided:** Avoided pie charts that make comparisons difficult.

---

## Category Profitability

**Business Question:** Which categories generate the highest profit?

**Chart Type:** Horizontal Bar Chart

**Reason:** Enables comparison of profit across categories.

**Fields Used**

* Category
* Profit

**Design Principle:** Consistent color and sorting.

**Mistake Avoided:** Avoided stacked charts that reduce readability.

---

## Customer Segment

**Business Question:** Which customer segment contributes most to business performance?

**Chart Type:** Bar Chart

**Reason:** Clearly compares revenue across customer groups.

**Fields Used**

* Customer Segment
* Sales
* Profit (Color)

**Design Principle:** Clear labels and consistent formatting.

**Mistake Avoided:** Avoided unnecessary decorative elements.

---

## Shipping Performance

**Business Question:** Which shipping mode results in longer delivery times?

**Chart Type:** Bar Chart

**Reason:** Easy comparison of average delivery days.

**Fields Used**

* Ship Mode
* Delivery Days

**Design Principle:** Simple comparison with readable labels.

**Mistake Avoided:** Avoided complex visualizations for operational metrics.

---

## Discount vs Profit

**Business Question:** How do discounts affect profitability?

**Chart Type:** Scatter Plot

**Reason:** Scatter plots effectively display relationships between two continuous variables.

**Fields Used**

* Discount
* Profit
* Category (Color)
* Sales (Size)

**Design Principle:** Highlights patterns and outliers.

**Mistake Avoided:** Avoided line charts because discount values are not sequential.

---

## Return Analysis

**Business Question:** Which products or segments experience higher return rates?

**Chart Type:** Bar Chart

**Reason:** Easy comparison of returned orders.

**Fields Used**

* Category
* Returned Orders
* Customer Segment (Color)

**Design Principle:** Clear comparison across business dimensions.

**Mistake Avoided:** Avoided overly complex heatmaps for executive reporting.
